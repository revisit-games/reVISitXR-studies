# reVISit-XR Live Demonstration

This is a live study demo of reVISit-XR. For testing through, we suggest you wear a virtual reality (VR) or augmented reality (AR) headset to experience the study. Our development and testing were using Meta Quest 3, and we welcome feedback on using other headsets.

 - Note: we suggest **maximizing** your browser window, as the views (in both the testing and replaying) can be on the larger side. Thank you!

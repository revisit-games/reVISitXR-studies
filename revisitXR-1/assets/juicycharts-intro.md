# PART II: Play the Infographics!

This is the second, as well as the main body, of this study. In this part, you will be randomly given a total of **7** Infographics/diagrams/charts. For **each** infographic, you may be **randomly assigned to** a **juicy feedback version** (such as animation, visual effects, graphic transitions, and sound effects), or a **non-juicy feedback version**. For each of the infographics, you will be asked to interact with them, gain the answer for tasks, and report your experience of using or playing them.

Whenever you finish your answer, you will be able to click the **[Next]** button, to move to the next page of the study. We suggest maximizing your browser window, as the infographics/diagrams/charts can be on the larger side.

---

## Note:

Each infographic has a **"*Name*"** shown in the top-left corner. At the end, we will ask about your experience with a specific infographic. Please try to notice/remember the "*Name*" so we know which one you mean. Thank you!

# PART II: Play the Game!

This is the second, as well as the main body, of this study. In this part, you will play and complete a total of 2 versions of a small video game -- one version with the juicy feedback design elements (the visual embellishments) and one without. In each of the versions of the game, you need to navigate blue cubes (you have 5 in total) across a busy road and river, avoid the red cubes, and reach the other side. **In each of the versions of the game, you will need to complete a total of 5 sessions** (therefore, for each version, there are 25 blue cubes in total that need to reach the other side). Whenever you finish a game level, you will be able to click the **[Next]** button, which is located at the bottom of the game screen, to move to the next page of the study. 


Below is a brief tutorial on how to play the game, and the game objective description about how to complete this part of the study. Please read below carefully. You will also be able to revisit this information at any time by clicking the **Help** button in the top-right corner of the screen. We suggest maximizing your browser window, as the video game can be on the larger side. Please make sure that you are using **Google Chrome** or **Microsoft Edge** browsers when participating in this study.

# Game Tutorial

Navigating your characters (the blue cubes) across the busy road and river! Avoiding the cars (red cubes)! And reach the other side! 

You will have **5 blue cubes for each session** that need to reach the other side (25 blue cubes in total), and you can navigate one of them for each time. You need to navigate all of 5 (one by one) to reach the other side to complete the game.

<img src="./assets/tutorial02_1.gif" width="auto" height="450px">

---

The following keys are for control of the blue cube:

**W**, **A**, **S**, **D** or **↑**, **↓**, **←**, **→**: Movement.

**Be careful!** Hitting a car (the red cube) might get you out of the game, and so could falling into water (the blue floor)!

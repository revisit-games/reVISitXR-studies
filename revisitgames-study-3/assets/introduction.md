# Evaluating Player Experience in Juicy Feedback of Games

Thank you for participating in this research study! 

This study aims to evaluate your playing experiences from playing two versions of a small video game -- one version with the juicy feedback design elements (the visual embellishments) and one without. 

The study has three sections: (1) The first will show you explanations of the study objectives and procedures, as well as a digital consent form; (2) the second section includes two versions of a small video game, in which you need to navigate blue cubes (in total of 5) to across a busy road and river, avoid the red cubes, and reach the other side; and (3) after finishing the game, the last section will show you a follow-up questionnaire that asks about your playing experiences.
